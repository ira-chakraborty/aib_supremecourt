Supreme Court: Beauty Contest

inspired by Alice in Borderland

Contestants choose a number between 0 and 100. The average of the numbers everyone selected is multiplied by 0.8. The person whose number is the closest to that result is the winner. The four remaining players each lose a point. A new rule is introduced for every player eliminated. #1 player eliminated: If two or more players choose the same number, the number they choose becomes invalid, meaning they lose a point even if the number is closest to the average multiplied by 0.8. #2 players eliminated: Choosing the exact correct number will cause other players to lose two points instead of one. #3 players eliminated: If one player chooses 0, the other player can win by choosing 100. It is GAME CLEAR for the last remaining player. It is GAME OVER for the player who reaches -10 points. Their scale above their head will overflow and pour aqua regia (an acid strong enough to dissolve gold) on them.
